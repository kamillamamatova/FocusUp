Settings

Exports/imports your full timer data as JSON. Use this to back up or manually transfer your data to another browser or device. When Notion is connected, history syncs automatically.

Timer controls

Start Begins counting up. Your time is saved automatically, closing the tab won't lose it.
Pause Freezes the timer. Hit Continue whenever you're ready to pick back up.
Reset Clears today's running total back to zero. Your history log is not affected.
Finish Day Locks in today's focus time, updates your streak, and resets the timer for tomorrow. Do this at the end of each day. If you connected Notion with a database selected, it also saves a row there automatically.
Goal Your daily target in minutes. The timer shows how much time is left and turns green when you hit overtime. A day counts toward your streak only if your logged minutes meet or exceed this goal.

Displays

Elapsed Total focused time so far today across all sessions.
Remaining Time left until you hit your goal. Counts down to zero then stops.
Overtime How long you've gone past your goal. Counts up once Remaining hits zero.

Account & sync

Guest mode No account needed. Your data is saved on the server tied to this browser. Clearing browser storage breaks the link, so use Export to back up if needed.
Account Sign up with an email and password (the person icon, top right). Lets you log in from any browser and keeps your history across devices without Notion.
Notion Sync Connects FocusUp to a Notion database. Every Finish Day adds a row with your date, minutes, goal, and whether you hit it, so your focus log lives inside Notion as a real table you can filter and annotate.
